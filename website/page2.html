My Work

For this app designing project we were tasked with recreating certain function on an app such as creating and managing a playlist. Before we designed the app on the computer we sketched out our ideas. We were able to come up with a clear, concise plan for our design. While discussing our ideas we heavily discussed many options and went back and forth a lot on ideas before settling on our final design plan..

Our final project Jamz, was a user-friendly music app that gave the user total control. Through creating, sorting, managing playlists, the user is able to control exactly how they want their music to be. With quick add music options such as Favorites, we believe that our app truly gives the user the ultimate user-friendly, efficient music experience they've been waiting for.

What I Learned

During this project I enjoyed many different aspects of designing our app. Making decisions centered around the user, and thinking about how users across the board would use our app was so fun and interesting. Getting into the mindset of what can best fit many different types of users was something new to me and I liked the design aspect because it allowed me to incorporate ideas I had for the apps that I used. There was a lot of power in being able to control the different aspects and features of our app and I realized that this process is something I loved doing. Deciding what content is necessary and what features best fit our original goal, but also adjusting and creating new ideas as we moved along on our project showed me that this is something I am interested in for my future.

Obstacles We Faced

Although I really enjoyed making our app, there were also a lot of difficult decisions we made. Having a favorites quick add option is not popular among many apps, but we added it because we thought it was efficient and effective in managing a playlist quickly. Just in terms of design, we went back and forth a lot in icons, navigation bar, and overall theme and page layout. The things we could have improved on our app were that we forgot to add a settings button and just in general, our app was designed similarly to Spotify's. Given more time we would have liked to change the layout a bit more .
